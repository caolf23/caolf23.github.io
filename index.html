Selected Projects

Deep Reinforcement Learning for Hex Grid Game

Reinforcement Learning · Game AI · PyTorch · Multi-GPU Training

Built a Gymnasium-compatible Hex grid game environment with configurable reward structures for two-agent self-play training.

  • Implemented PPO training with PyTorch DDP for multi-GPU acceleration on 4 A800 GPUs.
  • Developed Actor-Critic and MCTS-based training pipelines.
  • Designed a ResNet-style policy network with residual connections to capture board structure.
  • Used GAE and action masking to improve training stability and legal-action handling.
Reinforcement Learning PPO MCTS ResNet Policy Self-Play

Mathematical Contest in Modeling

Mathematical Modeling · Optimization · Unsupervised Learning

Developed a data-driven model for historical foot traffic and tourist-flow analysis using wear-pattern modeling and statistical learning.

  • Extended the Archard wear equation and applied simulated annealing for model fitting.
  • Used X-Means clustering and Z-score analysis for anomaly detection and maintenance-state classification.
  • Provided mathematical analysis of tread-edge and center-depression approximation behavior.
Mathematical Modeling Simulated Annealing Clustering Anomaly Detection

Video Generation Model Architecture Study

Generative Models · Diffusion Transformers · Empirical Evaluation

Studied modern video generation architectures, including the transition from U-Net-based diffusion models to Diffusion Transformers and variable-resolution patching strategies.

  • Analyzed Sora-style architectural design and the role of Diffusion Transformers.
  • Benchmarked Open-Sora on RTX 4090 hardware and studied prompt-complexity sensitivity.
  • Proposed directions combining differentiable physics and LLM-based temporal planning to improve physical consistency.
